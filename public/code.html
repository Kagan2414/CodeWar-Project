CodeWar 2.O

( National Event for First Year Engineering Students)

Join the thrilling Code War, a dynamic coding competition hosted by Park College of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore! Exclusively crafted for first-year engineering students, this event is your chance to ignite your passion for programming, unleash your creativity, and showcase your problem-solving skills. Dive into a fun yet challenging environment where young tech enthusiasts shine and coding dreams take flight!

BROCHURE

Rules & Regulations
Students

Eligibility

The event is open exclusively to first-year Engineering students from any branch.
Participants must present a valid college ID or proof of enrollment during registration.
Participation is allowed individually or in teams of up to four members.
For team participation, all members must meet the eligibility criteria.
Students

Team Formation

Competitors can participate as Individuals or as Teams.
A team can consist of minimum two members and maximum of four members. Exceptions cannot be made in case of extra team members.
All teams should have their own laptops and the committee will not provide any laptops.
Changes to the team members are not permitted after Friday (March 7). To make any changes contact our committee members.
Students

Registration

The event registrations must be completed online and on-spot registration is not available.
The last date for registration is March 15, 2025.
The competition will consist of two rounds: the first round will be held online on March 19, 2025, and the final round will be conducted offline at Park College of Engineering and Technology on February 28, 2025.
The registration fee is 200 per student, which includes refreshments and certificates.

Scope and Outcomes

Solving Innovative Problem Statement : In CodeWar V2.O, participants will tackle innovative problem statements designed to challenge their logical thinking and programming expertise, providing a platform to showcase their problem-solving skills and creativity.

Build Innovative Projects: Turn your ideas into reality! Develop cutting-edge software applications, websites, or even hardware prototypes during this exciting hackathon. Showcase your programming and problem-solving skills while creating tangible, impactful results.

Boost Your Skills: CodeWar V2.O is a golden opportunity to level up your technical expertise. Learn new programming languages, frameworks, and tools, while gaining hands-on experience in a collaborative environment. Sharpen your teamwork, communication, and time management skills as you work with peers.

Network and Collaborate: Meet and connect with first-year engineering students from different branches. Form teams, brainstorm ideas, and work together to create innovative solutions. This is your chance to exchange knowledge, build relationships, and grow your professional circle.

Unlock Career Opportunities: Stand out to industry sponsors, professionals, and mentors scouting for fresh talent. Exceptional projects and standout performances can open doors to internships, mentorship programs, and even job opportunities. Give your career a head start right from the first year!

Embrace Personal Growth: Step out of your comfort zone and push your limits in this intense yet rewarding hackathon. Gain confidence, overcome challenges, and cultivate a growth mindset. The immersive experience of CodeWar V2.O will shape you into a more skilled and resilient individual.



Join CodeWar V2.0 and embark on a thrilling journey of innovation, learning, and growth. Be part of a community that celebrates creativity and problem-solving. The stage is set for YOU!
